Just your friendly neighborhood Jack, still chasing the dream. Born in Detroit, now getting his start in Chicago — a recent Columbia College graduate with an interdisciplinary degree. Meaning he can do a little bit of everything.

Jack isn't just a composer. He's a sound designer, audio implementer, producer, and junior programmer, bringing energy to every project. He's currently interning as the lead composer at SZNL Media and at No More Heroes Studios, both Chicago based.

His musical education began back in the second grade when he started playing the cello. From there he grew a love for disco. Down the road, he started listening to the Grateful Dead in high school. Back in 2012, Jack went to his first music festival — Bonnaroo — sparking a deep appreciation for electronic music. Later in life came the discovery of Anatolian psychedelia and the golden grooves of Habibi Funk.
